# Pricing — AIWholesail

> Last updated: 2026-05-12
> Source of truth: https://aiwholesail.com/pricing
> Billing: monthly via Stripe (web) / RevenueCat (mobile). 7-day free trial, no credit card required.

AIWholesail is an AI-powered platform for real estate investors — wholesalers, flippers, buy-and-hold landlords, and out-of-state investors. Two plans, both with a 7-day free trial.

## Pro — $49/month

For new and growing investors who need core deal sourcing + analysis.

- **Price:** $49/month
- **Property searches:** 10 per day
- **Alert locations:** Up to 5
- **AI property analysis:** 10 per month
- **AI photo condition analysis:** 10 per month
- **AI-ranked comps with reasoning:** 25 per month
- **Skip tracing (owner phone / email / address):** 25 lookups per month
- **Buyer-pitch PDFs:** Unlimited
- **Webhooks:** Up to 3 endpoints
- **Included:** Deal pipeline, contract generator (Assignment, Purchase, LOI, Proof of Funds), sequences, all 14 free investment calculators
- **Trial:** 7-day free trial (no credit card required)

## Elite — $99/month

For active wholesalers, flippers, and full-time investors who need volume and depth.

- **Price:** $99/month
- **Property searches:** Unlimited
- **Alert locations:** Unlimited

- **AI property analysis:** Unlimited
- **AI photo condition analysis:** Unlimited
- **AI-ranked comps with reasoning:** Unlimited

- **Buyer-pitch PDFs:** Unlimited
- **Webhooks:** Unlimited endpoints

## What's NOT charged extra for

- Deal pipeline / CRM
- Contract generator
- Buyer database
- All 14 investment calculators (Wholesale Deal, BRRRR, Cap Rate, Rehab Estimator, MAO, ARV, Cash Flow, DSCR, etc.)
- State-law content, market pages, neighborhood data
- Branded buyer-pitch PDFs

## Billing details

- **Currency:** USD
- **Cycle:** Monthly, billed via Stripe (web) or RevenueCat (iOS/Android)
- **Cancellation:** Self-serve, anytime, takes effect at end of current cycle
- **Refunds:** Pro-rated refunds available on request within 7 days of charge — see https://aiwholesail.com/refund
- **Free trial:** 7 days, no credit card required for trial start; card collected when converting to paid

## Decision guide

- Pick **Pro** if you're sourcing under ~10 deals/month and primarily need analysis + scoring.
- Pick **Elite** if you analyze high volume, need AI photo vision on every property, or want unlimited skip tracing and comps.
