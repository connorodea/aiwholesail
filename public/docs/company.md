# About AIWholesail

> Last updated: 2026-05-12

## What we do

AIWholesail makes real estate investing tractable for people who don't have ten years of experience or twenty hours a week to spend analyzing properties. We surface profitable deals, score them with AI, help you reach owners, and generate the contracts to close — all in one platform.

## How we got here

The platform exists because the founder, Connor O'Dea, kept seeing the same gap: every existing tool solved one slice of the wholesaler / flipper workflow but nothing ran the whole loop. PropStream gave you data without analysis. DealMachine handled driving-for-dollars but stopped there. BatchLeads handled lists and skip tracing but didn't tell you which deals were worth doing. So you ended up paying three or four subscriptions and gluing them together in spreadsheets.

AIWholesail collapses that into one workflow with AI doing the heavy lifting on the analysis half — scoring deals 0–100, surfacing spread automatically from Zestimate, reading property condition from listing photos.

## What we believe

- **Software should do the math.** If a number can be calculated, it shouldn't require a 30-minute manual analysis per property. AI is good at this. We use it.
- **The score should explain itself.** A 0–100 deal score with no reasoning is a black box. Ours always shows the why.
- **No guru stuff.** No income guarantees, no stock-photo enthusiasm, no "passive income" mythology. The tool surfaces signals. You do the work of closing.
- **Honest pricing.** $49/mo or $99/mo, no contracts, no upsells, no enterprise dance.
- **Open data trumps gated content.** AI agents acting on behalf of investors should be able to read our pricing, features, and docs in machine-readable form. That's why this `/docs` surface exists.

## What we're not

- Not a wholesaling service — we don't source deals for you
- Not a coaching program — there are great coaches; we're a tool
- Not a guarantee — no software guarantees a closed deal
- Not bulk data — we lean toward analysis + workflow over raw exports
