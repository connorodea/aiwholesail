# AIWholesail Use Cases

> Last updated: 2026-05-12

How different real estate investors use AIWholesail, by persona and job-to-be-done.

## Active Wholesaler

**Goal:** Find motivated-seller deals, lock them under contract, assign to a cash buyer for a fee.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Set alerts on 5 target ZIP codes with distress-signal filters.
2. Each morning, review the 0–100 AI-scored deals with $30K+ spreads.
3. Skip-trace owners on the top 5 leads (Pro: 25/mo, Elite: 200/mo).
4. Send a sequence (text + email) from the platform.
5. When a seller engages, run the Wholesale Deal Calculator to confirm MAO.
6. Generate the Assignment + LOI contracts in-app.
7. Push the deal to the buyer database with a one-click branded buyer-pitch PDF.

**Why this beats current tools:** PropStream gives you data but no scoring or pitch PDF. BatchLeads gives you lists and skip traces but no analysis or contract. AIWholesail keeps the whole loop in one place.

## House Flipper

**Goal:** Buy under ARV, rehab accurately, sell at retail.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Search by ARV potential, not list price.
2. Use AI photo vision (Elite) to flag heavy-rehab properties before driving out.
3. Auto-fill the Rehab Estimator from listing photos and property age.
4. Pull AI-ranked comps to confirm ARV.
5. Generate Proof of Funds to win the offer.
6. Track from offer → close → renovation in the pipeline.

**Why this matters:** Photo vision and rehab estimation prevent the #1 flipper failure mode — underestimating repairs.

## Buy-and-Hold Landlord

**Goal:** Build a cash-flowing rental portfolio.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Filter for properties with positive cash flow at market rents.
2. Auto-fill Cap Rate, Cash Flow, DSCR, and 1% Rule calculators.
3. Compare 5–10 properties side-by-side.
4. Track financing options against DSCR thresholds.
5. Use BRRRR Calculator to model refinance-out scenarios.

## First-Time Investor

**Goal:** Find your first deal without overpaying.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Pick a market via the market guide pages.
2. Let AI scoring rank deals 0–100 — focus on 70+ scores.
3. Read the AI's plain-language reasoning to learn what makes a deal good.
4. Use free calculators at https://aiwholesail.com/tools without signing up.
5. Start a 7-day free trial when ready to act on a specific property.

**Why this matters:** The AI score is a teaching tool — it shows what experienced investors look for.

## Out-of-State Investor

**Goal:** Invest in markets you can't physically visit.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Pick markets by macro fundamentals (population, job growth) via market pages.
2. Use AI photo vision (Elite) to assess condition from listing photos.
3. Pull AI-ranked comps for ARV confidence.
4. Skip-trace local owners or buyer-match remotely.
5. Use the buyer-pitch PDF to coordinate with a local boots-on-the-ground partner.

## Real Estate Agent (Investor-Adjacent)

**Goal:** Win and serve investor clients.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Use AIWholesail to pre-qualify deals before recommending to investor clients.
2. Share branded buyer-pitch PDFs as client-facing collateral.
3. Use state-law content as client education.
4. Use deal pipeline to track investor-client opportunities.

## Cash Buyer

**Goal:** Curated, fit-for-criteria deal flow without sifting through bad wholesaler lists.

**Workflow with AIWholesail:**
1. Get added to wholesaler buyer databases through the platform.
2. Receive matched deals based on stated criteria (market, type, budget).
3. Use calculators to vet a wholesaler's numbers in seconds.

## Out-of-scope (anti-personas)

- **Pure data buyers** wanting bulk CSV exports — we lean toward analysis + workflow.
- **"Guaranteed leads"** seekers — we surface signals, not pre-vetted deals.
- **"Get rich quick"** seekers — the platform avoids guru tone and income guarantees.
