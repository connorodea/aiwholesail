## Common issues and self-serve answers

### "My free trial expired and I lost access"
The 7-day trial converts only if you add a payment method. If your trial expired without converting, your account is still there — just visit https://aiwholesail.com/pricing and pick a plan to restore access. Trial-period deal data is retained for 30 days.

### "I'm not getting deal alerts"
Check your alert locations in Settings → Alerts. Pro is capped at 5 locations; Elite is unlimited. Also check spam filters for `alerts@aiwholesail.com`.

### "My skip-trace credits show 0"
Skip-trace credits reset on your billing cycle date. Pro: 25/mo. Elite: 200/mo. Unused credits do not roll over. Email connor@aiwholesail.com if you need a one-time top-up.

### "Stripe charged me but my plan didn't activate"
Sign out and back in. If the plan still hasn't activated, email connor@aiwholesail.com with the Stripe receipt — we'll fix it manually within hours.

### "I want a refund"
Pro-rated refunds within 7 days of charge. Email connor@aiwholesail.com with the reason. Full policy: https://aiwholesail.com/refund

### "I can't cancel"
Cancel from Account → Subscription, or email connor@aiwholesail.com. We will not block cancellation. If your card is being charged after cancellation, send the Stripe receipt and we'll refund immediately.

### "Mobile app isn't syncing with web"
Force-quit the app and reopen. If syncing is still off, sign out and back in on both surfaces. Subscriptions purchased via the mobile native app go through RevenueCat — make sure the email matches between web and app accounts.

### "I want to integrate AIWholesail with my CRM"
Webhooks are available on both plans (Pro: 3 endpoints, Elite: unlimited). API access is on request — email connor@aiwholesail.com with your use case.

## Reporting bugs

Send to connor@aiwholesail.com with:
- What you did (the steps)
- What you expected
- What actually happened
- Browser + OS, or app version + device if mobile
- Screenshot or screen recording if possible

## Feature requests

Same email — connor@aiwholesail.com. Founder reads every one. The fastest way to influence the roadmap is to explain *what you're trying to accomplish* and *what's blocking you today*, not just the feature you want.

## What support cannot help with

- Investment / legal advice on specific deals (consult a local real estate attorney)
- State-by-state legality of wholesaling — see https://aiwholesail.com/state-laws as a starting point, then consult counsel
- Refunds for non-AIWholesail charges (we only handle our own Stripe and RevenueCat charges)
