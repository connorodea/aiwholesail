# Real Estate Investing Glossary

> Last updated: 2026-05-12
> AIWholesail's working glossary of terms used across the platform and content.

## A

**ARV (After-Repair Value)** — The estimated market value of a property after renovations are complete. Used to set the maximum allowable offer (MAO) and judge deal viability. AIWholesail estimates ARV using AI-ranked comparable sales.

**Assignment of Contract** — A contract in which a wholesaler transfers their right to purchase a property to an end buyer, typically for an assignment fee. AIWholesail generates Assignment contracts with one click.

**Assignment Fee** — The amount a wholesaler is paid for assigning their purchase contract to a cash buyer. Typically $5,000–$15,000, sometimes higher.

**Absentee Owner** — A property owner who does not live in the property. Often a target for wholesalers because absentee owners are more likely to sell.

## B

**BRRRR** — Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. A buy-and-hold strategy that recycles capital by refinancing out after value-add renovation. AIWholesail's BRRRR Calculator models the full cycle.

**Buyer-Pitch PDF** — A branded one-pager AIWholesail generates per deal to hand off to cash buyers, summarizing the property, spread, ARV, and rehab estimate.

## C

**Cap Rate (Capitalization Rate)** — Net operating income (NOI) divided by purchase price. A measure of a rental property's return independent of financing. AIWholesail auto-fills the Cap Rate Calculator from property data.

**Cash Buyer** — An investor who buys properties with cash (no financing contingency). Wholesalers maintain cash-buyer lists for fast assignments.

**Cash Flow** — Monthly rental income minus all expenses (mortgage, taxes, insurance, maintenance, vacancy reserve, management). Positive cash flow is the goal for buy-and-hold landlords.

**Comparable Sales (Comps)** — Recently sold properties similar in size, condition, and location to the subject property. Used to estimate ARV. AIWholesail's AI ranks comps with reasoning.

**Contract Generator** — AIWholesail's in-app document generator for Assignment, Purchase, LOI, and Proof of Funds.

## D

**Deal Score** — AIWholesail's 0–100 AI-generated rating of a property's wholesaling / flipping potential, with a plain-language explanation.

**DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio)** — Net operating income divided by total debt service. Lenders use DSCR to qualify rental-property loans. AIWholesail's DSCR Calculator models this for refi scenarios.

**Distress Signals** — Indicators that a property owner may be motivated to sell at a discount: pre-foreclosure, tax delinquency, code violations, vacancies, probate, divorce, eviction filings.

**Driving for Dollars (D4D)** — Driving neighborhoods to identify distressed properties visually. Competitor DealMachine specializes in this motion.

## E

**End Buyer** — The cash buyer to whom a wholesaler assigns their purchase contract.

**Earnest Money** — Deposit a buyer makes when going under contract, typically refundable until inspection or appraisal contingencies expire.

## F

**Fix-and-Flip** — Buy distressed → renovate → sell at retail. Profit = sale price − purchase price − rehab cost − holding cost − closing costs.

**Foreclosure** — Process by which a lender takes possession of a property after the borrower defaults. Pre-foreclosure (notice of default filed) is a common wholesaling target.

## H

**Holding Costs** — Costs incurred while owning a property before sale: mortgage, taxes, insurance, utilities, HOA. Flippers track holding costs against time-to-sale.

## L

**LOI (Letter of Intent)** — A non-binding letter expressing intent to purchase at stated terms, often used to test seller willingness before drafting a full purchase agreement. AIWholesail generates LOIs.

## M

**MAO (Maximum Allowable Offer)** — The highest price an investor can pay for a property and still hit their target margin. Common formula: `(ARV × 0.70) − rehab cost − wholesale fee`. AIWholesail's MAO Calculator handles this.

**Motivated Seller** — A property owner with a strong reason to sell quickly, often at a discount. Identifying motivated sellers is core to wholesaling.

**MLS (Multiple Listing Service)** — Regional databases of properties listed for sale by licensed agents.

## O

**Off-Market** — Properties not currently listed on the MLS. Often acquired via direct-to-seller outreach (calls, mail, texts).

**Owner-Finance** — Seller-financed transaction in which the seller acts as the lender, common in creative-finance strategies.

## P

**Pre-Foreclosure** — Period after a Notice of Default has been filed but before the foreclosure sale. A common wholesaling target.

**Proof of Funds (POF)** — Document proving the buyer has the cash or financing to close. Required by many sellers and listing agents. AIWholesail generates POF letters in-app.

**Purchase Agreement (PA)** — The binding contract between buyer and seller for the sale of real property.

## R

**REI (Real Estate Investor)** — Generic term for anyone investing in real estate, including wholesalers, flippers, landlords, and developers.

**Rehab** — Renovation work to bring a property to retail-rentable or retail-saleable condition.

**Rehab Estimator** — AIWholesail's calculator that estimates renovation cost by line item. The Elite plan's AI photo vision can populate estimates from listing photos.

**REO (Real Estate Owned)** — Property owned by a lender after a failed foreclosure auction.

## S

**Skip Tracing** — Looking up current contact information for a property owner — phone, email, address. AIWholesail includes 25/mo (Pro) or 200/mo (Elite) skip traces.

**Spread** — The gap between list price (or contract price) and ARV. AIWholesail surfaces properties with $30K+ spreads automatically.

**Subject-To** — A creative-finance strategy in which a buyer takes title while leaving the existing mortgage in the seller's name.

## T

**Tax Delinquency** — Property with overdue property tax. A distress signal that often indicates a motivated seller.

## V

**Vacancy Signal** — Indicator that a property is unoccupied (no utility usage, mail accumulation, code violations). A distress signal.

## W

**Wholesale Deal** — A transaction in which a wholesaler contracts a property and assigns the contract to an end buyer for an assignment fee, without taking title.

**Wholesale Deal Calculator** — AIWholesail's tool to model a wholesale deal end-to-end: ARV → MAO → assignment fee → buyer math.

**Wholesaling** — Real estate strategy of contracting properties and assigning the contracts for an assignment fee. Legality and disclosure rules vary by state — see https://aiwholesail.com/state-laws

## Z

**Zestimate** — Zillow's automated valuation model (AVM) for residential property. AIWholesail uses Zestimate as the market-value anchor for spread calculation.
