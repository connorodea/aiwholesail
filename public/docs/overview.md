# AIWholesail Overview

> Last updated: 2026-05-12
> Source of truth: this file and https://aiwholesail.com

## What it is

AIWholesail is an AI-powered platform that helps real estate investors find, analyze, and close profitable deals. It combines deal sourcing, AI scoring, owner skip tracing, comp analysis, contract generation, and a deal pipeline in one workflow.

## What it does

1. **Find deals** — Scans thousands of properties across all 50 U.S. states and surfaces those with a profitable spread (Zestimate − list price ≥ $30K).
2. **Analyze deals** — Auto-fills 14 investment calculators (Wholesale Deal, BRRRR, Cap Rate, MAO, Rehab Estimator, ARV, DSCR, Cash Flow, and others) from property data.
3. **Score deals** — Assigns each property a 0–100 AI deal score with a plain-language explanation.
4. **Reach owners** — Built-in skip tracing returns owner phone, email, and address (Pro: 25/mo, Elite: 200/mo).
5. **Close deals** — One-click contract generator (Assignment, Purchase Agreement, Letter of Intent, Proof of Funds) and a deal pipeline with sequences and buyer matching.
6. **Hand off** — Branded buyer-pitch PDFs let wholesalers hand a deal to their assignment buyer in one click.

## Who it's for

- **Active wholesalers** — primary persona; deal volume, speed, and assignment fees
- **House flippers** — ARV accuracy and rehab-budget confidence
- **Buy-and-hold landlords** — cash-flow-positive properties at scale
- **First-time investors** — AI scoring removes the experience gap
- **Out-of-state investors** — AI photo vision + remote analysis compresses the "I can't visit the property" friction
- **Real estate agents** — investor-focused tools they can use with clients

## Why it exists

Good deals get bought in hours. Manually analyzing properties takes 30+ minutes each. The investor who calculates spread fastest wins. Existing tools each solve one slice — PropStream is a data warehouse, DealMachine is driving-for-dollars only, BatchLeads is skip-tracing-plus-lists, BiggerPockets is education. None run the full lifecycle. AIWholesail does.
