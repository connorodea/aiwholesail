## What to include in a first email

The fastest replies come from emails that include:

1. **Who you are** (one sentence)
2. **What you want** (one sentence)
3. **What you've already tried** (if relevant)
4. **Any deadline** (if relevant)

Skip slide decks and intro calls on round one. We open every email, but the ones with a clear ask move first.

## What we won't reply to

- Mass cold outreach (sequence pitches, SEO services, "I'll write blog posts for backlinks")
- Threats, abuse, or scams
- "Hey, jumping back to the top of your inbox" with no new information
