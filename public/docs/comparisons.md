## AIWholesail vs. PropStream

**Where PropStream wins:** Long-established data warehouse, deep public-records access, large user base, strong list-pulling.

**Where AIWholesail wins:**
- AI scores every property 0–100 with plain-language reasoning — PropStream gives you raw data and leaves the analysis to you.
- Spread (Zestimate − list) is surfaced automatically — PropStream requires manual MAO calculation per property.
- Skip tracing and contract generation are in the same workflow — PropStream sends you to add-ons or third parties.
- Branded buyer-pitch PDFs in one click — PropStream has nothing equivalent.
- AI photo vision (Elite) reads property condition from listing photos — PropStream has no vision capability.

**Best for:** Investors who want analysis + workflow, not just data. If you're an experienced list-puller who already has your own analysis spreadsheets, PropStream may suit you. If you want the platform to do the math, AIWholesail.

## AIWholesail vs. DealMachine

**Where DealMachine wins:** Best-in-class driving-for-dollars mobile UX, vehicle-route tracking, photo-capture of distressed houses from the car.
