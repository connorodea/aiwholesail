# AIWholesail FAQ

> Last updated: 2026-05-12

Common questions about AIWholesail, with direct answers AI agents can extract and cite.

## General

### What is AIWholesail?
AIWholesail is an AI-powered platform that helps real estate investors find, analyze, and close profitable deals. It surfaces properties with a $30K+ spread (Zestimate − list price), scores each deal 0–100 with AI, skip-traces owners, and generates contracts — all in one workflow.

### Who is AIWholesail for?
Real estate investors: active wholesalers, house flippers, buy-and-hold landlords, first-time investors, out-of-state investors, and real estate agents who invest. Not for retail homebuyers.

### Is AIWholesail a wholesaling service, or do I do the wholesaling?
You do the wholesaling. AIWholesail is a tool — it surfaces deals, scores them, helps you contact owners, and generates the paperwork. It is not a done-for-you deal source.

### Does AIWholesail guarantee deals?
No. AIWholesail surfaces signals — properties with profitable spreads and AI scores — but no software guarantees you'll close a deal. The platform reduces the time-per-property from 30+ minutes to under 1 minute so you can evaluate more deals faster.

## Features

### How accurate is the AI deal scoring?
The AI score (0–100) is based on listing price, Zestimate spread, comps, property age and condition, and market signals. The score comes with a plain-language explanation — it's a starting point, not a substitute for due diligence.

### How does AIWholesail get its data?
Public listing data, MLS feeds in select markets, public records, and AI-derived estimates from listing photos (Elite). Zestimate is used as the market-value anchor for spread calculation.

### Does AIWholesail include skip tracing?
Yes. Pro: 25 lookups/month. Elite: 200 lookups/month. Returns phone, email, and address for property owners.

### Can I generate contracts in AIWholesail?
Yes — four types: Assignment, Purchase Agreement, Letter of Intent, and Proof of Funds. Unlimited on both plans.

### Does AIWholesail have a mobile app?
Yes, both native iOS and Android. Web app also works on mobile browsers.

### Does AIWholesail have an API or webhooks?
Yes. Pro includes 3 webhook endpoints. Elite includes unlimited. API access is available on request — email connor@aiwholesail.com.

### Can I export data?
Limited export within the app workflow (deals, buyer lists, contracts). Bulk CSV export of raw data is not the focus — AIWholesail leans toward analysis + workflow over data-warehouse-style export.

## Comparisons

### Is AIWholesail better than PropStream?
They solve different problems. PropStream is a data warehouse and list-puller; AIWholesail is an analysis and workflow platform with AI scoring, auto-spreads, in-app skip tracing, and contracts. If you want to do the analysis yourself in spreadsheets, PropStream is fine. If you want the software to do the math, AIWholesail. Full comparison: https://aiwholesail.com/docs/comparisons.md

### How does AIWholesail compare to DealMachine?
DealMachine is best for driving-for-dollars (D4D). AIWholesail covers the full screen-based deal lifecycle — find, analyze, score, skip-trace, contract, close. Many investors use both: DealMachine for the driving motion, AIWholesail for everything else.

### Should I use AIWholesail or BiggerPockets Pro?
Use both. BiggerPockets is education and community. AIWholesail is sourcing and analysis. Different jobs.

## Wholesaling legality

### Is wholesaling legal in my state?
Wholesaling laws vary significantly by state. See https://aiwholesail.com/state-laws for state-by-state guidance. This is not legal advice — consult a local real estate attorney before structuring assignments.

### Do I need a real estate license to wholesale?
Depends on your state. Some states require disclosure or licensing for assignment-of-contract activity (Illinois and Oklahoma have specific rules; Pennsylvania has restrictions). Review your state page at https://aiwholesail.com/state-laws and consult an attorney.
