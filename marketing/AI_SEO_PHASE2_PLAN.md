## Quora answer template (Tier 1, move #3)

> [Question: "How do I find motivated sellers?"]
>
> Three signal sources that compound:
>
> **1. Distress lists** — pre-foreclosure, tax-delinquent, vacant, absentee-owner, code-violation, probate. County recorder websites publish most of these free. Services like PropStream ($99/mo), BatchLeads (now part of PropStream), or AIWholesail ($49/mo, my company) aggregate them.
>
> **2. Direct mail** — 1-3% response rate on a cold list. Yellow letters pull slightly higher. Plan to send 7-12 touches over 30-60 days; single-send campaigns fail.
>
> **3. Driving for dollars** — manual but free. Photo distressed houses, look up owners via county records or DealMachine.
>
> Stack two of these (e.g., absentee-owner + tax-delinquent) and you'll find owners other investors haven't already mailed.
>
> The fastest learners I see book their first deal in 60-90 days from consistent action. Most failures are inconsistency, not strategy.
