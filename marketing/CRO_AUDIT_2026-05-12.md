## Site-wide CRO patterns to fix

1. **No exit-intent on key pages.** A modest "Get the 10-step motivated-seller checklist" exit-intent on `/guides/finding-motivated-sellers` (already the highest-value content page) could capture 3-8% of bounces as email leads. See `/popup-cro` skill.

2. **No mid-page CTA on long-form content.** The guides + blog posts are 10K+ words — only a footer CTA. A sticky bottom-bar "Start your 7-day trial" appearing after 30% scroll would lift content-page conversion 2-4x based on standard pattern data.

3. **No social proof on /vs/[competitor] pages.** Direct comparison pages are commercial-intent — the highest-converting cluster in B2B SaaS — but we have ZERO social proof on them. Adding a "X investors switched from PropStream this year" stat (real number, no placeholder) would be a 10-min change with measurable lift.

4. **Pricing not visible on home above-fold.** Marketing-context says "honest pricing" is a brand value. A "$49/mo or $99/mo · 7-day free trial" line in the hero subtitle removes a click from the funnel.

5. **No trust badges** (Stripe, GDPR, security). Even if internal — "Built on Stripe + Supabase" mentions the recognizable infrastructure brands and reassures wary investors.
