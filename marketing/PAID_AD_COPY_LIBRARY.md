## Measurement

Tag every campaign with UTM:
- `utm_source=` google / meta / tiktok / reddit
- `utm_medium=` cpc / paid_social / video
- `utm_campaign=` rsa_wholesaling / sq_founder_voice_v1 / etc.
- `utm_content=` headline + description variant identifier

Funnel from ad click → trial → paid. Aim for:
- Click → trial: ≥10% (cold traffic), ≥25% (retargeting)
- Trial → paid: 30% across all sources (per marketing-context target)
- Blended CPA: under $50 for Pro, under $120 for Elite

Beyond breakeven CPA, the marketing-context says target $10M ARR by Nov 2026. At a 30% trial-to-paid and ~$60 blended price, payback period is 1.7 months — anything under that is profitable to scale.
