## Executive Summary

This report contains 70 keywords across 10 categories, ranked by **Opportunity Score** (best ROI opportunities first). The scoring weights: low CPC (40%), decent search volume (30%), low competition (20%), and relevance to AIWholesail.com (10%).

## CATEGORY BREAKDOWN & STRATEGY

### 1. Real Estate Wholesaling Software (6 keywords)
**Best keywords:** real estate wholesaling ($27.50), property wholesaling ($3.58), wholesale investing ($3.80)
**Strategy:** These are your CORE keywords. Bid aggressively on exact match. Build dedicated landing pages for each.
**Budget allocation:** 25% of ad spend

### 2. Investment Property Finding Tools (20 keywords)
**Best keywords:** investment property for sale ($2.83), land investors ($2.22), good real estate investments ($3.88)
**Strategy:** Broad funnel. Use these to attract investors who need tools. Retarget with software-specific ads.
**Budget allocation:** 20% of ad spend

### 3. Real Estate Deal Analysis Software (6 keywords)
**Best keywords:** real estate investment analysis ($5), property investment strategies ($6.67), subject to investing ($5.45)
**Strategy:** Position AIWholesail as the deal analysis platform. Target exact match + phrase match.
**Budget allocation:** 10% of ad spend

### 4. Off-Market Property Leads (4 keywords)
**Best keywords:** off market real estate deals ($40), buying pre-foreclosure properties ($7.50)
**Strategy:** Higher CPC but directly relevant. Use sparingly with high-converting landing pages.
**Budget allocation:** 5% of ad spend

### 5. Real Estate CRM/Pipeline Tools (8 keywords)
**Best keywords:** investors realty ($2.96), local reia ($3.93), property investment company ($5.41)
**Strategy:** Position as investor CRM. Target REIA members and communities.
**Budget allocation:** 10% of ad spend

### 6. Property Analysis Calculators (3 keywords)
**Best keywords:** cap rate calculator ($27.50), real estate ROI calculator ($25)
**Strategy:** Build FREE calculators as lead magnets. Gate results behind email capture. These keywords have LOW competition because most advertisers sell leads, not tools.
**Budget allocation:** 10% of ad spend

### 7. Motivated Seller Leads (1 keyword in safe range)
**Best keyword:** real estate investors near me ($13.11)
**Strategy:** AVOID most motivated seller keywords (extremely expensive). Target the software buyer, not the seller.
**Budget allocation:** 5% of ad spend

### 8. Skip Tracing (0 keywords in list -- use Display/Video instead)
**Strategy:** Skip tracing keywords have low organic volume but high intent. Use Display ads targeting skip tracing tool comparison pages and YouTube pre-roll on skip tracing tutorials. CPC: $0.10-0.63.
**Budget allocation:** 5% of ad spend (Display/Video only)

### 9. Real Estate Investing for Beginners (12 keywords)
**Best keywords:** RE investing for beginners ($4.04, 55K vol), wholesaling for beginners ($4.07, 6.8K vol), RE investing 101 ($3.88, 6.1K vol)
**Strategy:** HIGHEST ROI CATEGORY. Massive volume, dirt-cheap CPC. Build educational content funnel: blog -> free tool -> trial -> paid.
**Budget allocation:** 10% of ad spend (but expect highest volume)

### 10. Flip Houses Software (2 keywords)
