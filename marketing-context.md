# AIWholesail Marketing Context

> Last updated: 2026-05-09 (post-Skip-Tracing / POF / Tools-tab releases). Re-baseline pricing, personas, and competitive moat.

## 1. Product Overview
- **One-line:** AI-powered platform that helps real estate investors find, analyze, and close profitable deals.
- **What it does:** AIWholesail scans thousands of properties, calculates profit spreads using Zestimate data, scores deals 0-100 with AI, and runs the full deal lifecycle — find motivated sellers → analyze with auto-filled calculators → skip-trace owners → manage pipeline → generate contracts.
- **Product category:** Real Estate Investing Software / PropTech
- **Product type:** SaaS (subscription, monthly billing via Stripe). Mobile native paywall via RevenueCat.
- **Pricing:** **Pro $49/mo** (10 searches/day, 5 alert locations, basic matching, basic analytics) · **Elite $99/mo** (unlimited searches + locations, advanced AI, AI photo analysis, skip tracing, lead scoring, ARV/comps, priority support). 7-day free trial, no CC required.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Target individuals:** Active wholesalers (primary), house flippers, buy-and-hold investors, first-time investors, real estate agents who invest. Out-of-state investors over-index because the AI vision + remote analysis features compress the "I can't visit the property" friction.
- **Primary use case:** Find undervalued properties with profitable spreads ($30K+) before competitors, then act on them faster.
- **Jobs to be done:**
  1. **Find deals:** scan markets, identify properties priced below market
  2. **Analyze deals:** ARV, comps, cash flow, rehab costs, ROI — pre-filled from property data
  3. **Contact owners:** skip-trace phones, emails, addresses for off-market outreach
  4. **Manage deals:** pipeline from lead → contract → close, sequences, buyer matching

## 4. Problems & Pain Points
- **Core challenge:** Good deals get bought in hours; manually analyzing properties takes 30+ minutes each. The investor who calculates spread fastest wins.
- **Current solutions fall short:**
  - PropStream → data warehouse, no AI scoring or spread surfacing
  - DealMachine → driving-for-dollars only
  - BatchLeads → lead lists + skip tracing, but no analysis or scoring
  - BiggerPockets → education and forums, not tools
  - Zillow/Redfin → built for consumers, not investors (no spread, no pipeline)
- **Cost of not solving:** Missing 5-10 deals/month, overpaying $20-50K when ARV math is wrong, 20+ hours/week on manual research that AI can do in minutes
- **Emotional tension:** Fear of making a bad deal, overwhelm from listings volume, frustration watching deals slip to faster operators
