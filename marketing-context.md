# AIWholesail Marketing Context

## 1. Product Overview
- **One-line:** AI-powered platform that helps real estate investors find, analyze, and close profitable deals.
- **What it does:** AIWholesail scans thousands of properties, calculates profit spreads using Zestimate data, scores deals 0-100 with AI, and provides tools for the full deal lifecycle — from finding motivated sellers to closing contracts.
- **Product category:** Real Estate Investing Software / PropTech
- **Product type:** SaaS (subscription)
- **Pricing:** Pro $29/mo (10 searches/day, basic features), Elite $99/mo (unlimited, all AI features). 7-day free trial, no CC required.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Target individuals:** Real estate investors (all levels), wholesalers, house flippers, landlords, buy-and-hold investors, real estate agents who invest
- **Primary use case:** Find undervalued properties with profitable spreads ($30K+) before competitors
- **Jobs to be done:**
  1. Find deals: scan markets, identify properties priced below value
  2. Analyze deals: calculate ARV, comps, cash flow, rehab costs, ROI
  3. Manage deals: track pipeline from lead to close, follow-up sequences, contracts

## 4. Problems & Pain Points
- **Core challenge:** Finding profitable deals is hard — good deals get snapped up in hours, and manually analyzing properties takes forever
- **Current solutions fall short:** PropStream gives data but no AI analysis. DealMachine is for driving-for-dollars only. BiggerPockets is education, not tools. None calculate spreads automatically.
- **Cost of not solving:** Missing deals, overpaying for properties, spending 20+ hours/week on manual research
- **Emotional tension:** Fear of making a bad deal, overwhelm from too many listings, frustration watching deals slip away

## 6. Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Confident but not arrogant. Professional but approachable. Data-driven but human.
- **Reading level:** 8th grade. No jargon without explanation.
- **Do:** Use specific numbers ($30K+ spreads, 264 markets). Reference real strategies (BRRRR, wholesaling). Be direct about what the tool does.
- **Don't:** Sound like a guru. Make income guarantees. Use exclamation marks excessively. Be condescending to beginners.
- **Keywords to use:** profitable deals, deal scoring, AI-powered, spread, below market value, investment opportunity
- **Keywords to avoid:** wholesale (in generic marketing — keep for technical context), get rich, passive income scheme
